Follow the steps to make a good cup of Tea

You need 5 minutes to prepare perfect Indian milk tea or chai. This is how I like mine!!!

Step 1

Heat 100 ml of water with generous quantity of crushed ginger (about 1 tsp) and 1 tsp of tea leaves.

Step 2

After it comes to a rolling boil, add 150 ml of skimmed or toned milk as per choice.

Step 3

When it comes to boil, add a tsp of sugar and let it simmer for 2 to 3 minutes. You can use honey or jaggery or sugarfree in place of sugar.

Step 4

Strain into a cup and enjoy the goodness
